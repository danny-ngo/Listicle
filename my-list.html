5 Essentials you should have in your wardrobe

Danny Ngo

Last updated Jan 7, 2019

What's up guys, Danny here. Today I'm gonna talk about 5 essential items you should have in your closet. These items are the foundation of every man's wardrobe and should be covered before experimenting with trendier styles. Sound interesting? Let's hop into it!

1.

Solid T-Shirts

J. Crew

A clean, simple, and timeless piece, this is a classic must-have in every man's wardrobe. Make sure it fits right and flatters your body shape.

Recommendation: White, black, and grey

2.

Denim Jeans

Levi's

Needless to say, a super versatile pair of pants you can wear with many outfits, on many occasions.

Recommendation: Indigo and black

3.

Hoodies

Amazon

A super comfortable piece, great to wear when the weather starts to get chilly. Either slightly oversized, or a muscle fit would work.

Recommendation: Grey, navy

4.

A Cool Jacket

Zara

Every guy needs a stylish jacket. Layering a jackets with a t-shirt or hoodie will elevate your style.

Recommendation: Bomber, denim, or leather

5.

Plaid Flannels

American Eagle

A very stylish piece perfect for layering on top of other essentials. There are many prints and colours to choose from, so express your personal taste.

Recommendation: Red and black, black and white, olive green and black, etc.
